About Us

At foodee we are dedicated to capturing the honest flavours that are found in the streets and alleyways of Vietnam. We use the same everyday ingredients available to ordinary Vietnamese to create authentic Vietnamese street fare. By keeping it simple and straightforward, we preserve the genuine local flavours that the locals would expect. Our dishes harmoniously blend together the five elements of sweet (earth), salty (water), sour (wood), spicy (metal) and bitter (fire). Common ingredients that feature in our dishes include lemongrass, Vietnamese mint, coriander, fish sauce, shrimp paste and lime.Generous in the use of fresh herbs and vegetables, our dishes will please the palates of both new comers and fans of Vietnamese cuisine.
